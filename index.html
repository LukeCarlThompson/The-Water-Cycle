The Water Cycle

Lets find out where
the rain comes from

To find out
we are going to learn
about the water cycle
Rain falls to the ground
and runs downhill.

As the water flows downhill
some of it soaks into
the ground where trees use it to grow.
Let's follow the flow of water further down.

some info about trees absorbing water

The water joins rivers and streams
which flow into large bodies of water
such as lakes or the ocean.

some info about the ocean being salty

When the sun shines down
it warms everything up.
Which causes the water to evaporate
and rise up into the sky.

some info about how water turns to vapor

As the water vapor rises
it cools down and groups.
together to form clouds.

some info about how water forms clouds

Wind blows the clouds
to other parts of the world.
Where the water vapor condenses back into water droplets
and fall down as rain to start the water cycle again.
